Things you will see in this document:
  • When to use At
  • When to use In
  • When to use On

At

1 - it is used to say the time:

a) My class starts at 5 p.m.

b) We play soccer at 9 a.m.


2 - it is used to indicate a place:

a) I work at SuperCoolCompany.

b) They study at Ninja University.


3 - it is used to indicate an activity:

a) He is very good at soccer.

b) They are good at programming.


In

1 - it is used to indicate months, seasons of the year, and times of the day:

a) This event will happen in November.

b) I love to walk outside in the spring.

c) We have breakfast in the morning.

d) They have lunch in the afternoon.

e) I see the sunset in the evening.


EXCEPTION

We use at night:

She likes to go out at night.

They sleep at night.


2 - it is used for name of cities or countries:

a) He lives in Rio de Janeiro.

b) I was born in Spain.

On

1 - it is used for the names of the weekdays:

a) I play soccer on Tuesday.

b) He goes to the bar on Friday.


1

2 - On the top of something

a) The book is on the table.

b) The pillow is on the couch.
