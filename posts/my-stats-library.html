My stats library

I have been interested in statistics for some time. I studied the basic classes in school, but I didn't go any deeper into stats, nor built my own statistic library using any programming language. So this is where I start this library. I want to learn more about statistics and how to use it to solve real world problems. I found some good content on the internet, and decided to give it a try. I know this knowledge is of great value, and of broad and powerful use.

What is statistics?

Statistics is the name given to the exercise of analyzing, interpreting, and making decisions based on data. It involves numbers, graphs, calculations, and a lot of interpretation.

Why is it important to study this?

We are exposed to a lot of data on a day to day basis. Knowing how to analyze and make decisions from it helps us to not be fools, and to better understand the world that surrounds us.

Finally, I think that statistics is the way to make scientific thinking take form. It is about discovering, exploring, and being curious.
